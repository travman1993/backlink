2. What Information We Collect

A. Information You Voluntarily Provide

When you contact us through our contact form or email, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message content
  • Any additional information you choose to share

This information is only used to respond to your inquiry and is kept confidential.

B. Automatically Collected Information

Analytics: We may use analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our sites. This helps us improve user experience.

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Referring websites
  • Browser type and device information
  • General location (city/region level, not exact)

Cookies: We use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and user preferences. See our Cookie Policy for details.

C. Tool Input Data

When you use our calculators and tools (tip calculator, bill splitter, etc.), we typically do not store your data. These tools work locally in your browser:

  • No information is sent to our servers
  • No data is permanently stored
  • All calculations happen on your device

Note: Some tools may collect anonymized usage data to improve functionality, but personal data is never saved.

We use analytics to understand user behavior and improve our offerings. This data is aggregated and never linked to your identity.

A. How We Protect Your Data

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted
