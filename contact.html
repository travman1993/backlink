Frequently Asked Questions

How can I report a bug?

Use the contact form and select "Bug Report" as the subject. Please include details about what tool had the issue, what happened, and what you expected to happen. The more detail, the better we can help!

Can I suggest a new tool?

Absolutely! We love hearing ideas. Submit your suggestion through the form with "Tool Suggestion" as the subject, and explain what problem your idea would solve. We review all suggestions and may reach out with questions.

Is there a phone number I can call?

We primarily communicate via email to ensure timely responses and documentation of requests. Email us at contact@dailyhelphub.com for the fastest response.

Do you offer partnerships or sponsorships?

We're open to discussing partnerships, collaborations, and sponsorship opportunities. Contact us with "Partnership" as the subject line and we'll get back to you.

How do I stay updated on new tools?

Check back to the Daily Help Hub homepage regularly for new additions. You can also follow us on social media or subscribe to updates (if available).

Are my messages private?

Yes, all messages are treated confidentially. We only use your information to respond to your inquiry. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
