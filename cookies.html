This Cookie Policy explains how Daily Help Hub uses cookies and similar technologies.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites work efficiently and provide information to website owners.

2. Why We Use Cookies

Daily Help Hub uses cookies for:

  • Site Functionality: Remember your preferences
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns
  • Performance: Analyze visitor interactions
  • Security: Protect against fraud

3. Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, delete, and block cookies.

Note: Disabling cookies may affect functionality of some tools.
